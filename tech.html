My Journey Into Tech

Published on Aug 29, 2025

Hi, I’m Keyla Welcome — and I’m a mother, builder, dreamer, and woman of faith who’s learning to code her way into a better future. I created this blog to share my journey — not because I have all the answers, but actually... because I don't . See I know what it’s like to feel like your dreams are too big, too late, too hard, too impossible, or too far out of reach. They’re not. You will be suprised to what you can achieve in the face of adversity. I’ve always dreamed of building smart things — a journal app, a smart house, maybe even a robot one day. I want to see what’s really possible with AI and tech, not just read about it then be afraid of it. I want to be a part of the movement. More than that, I want to create things that help others — especially other women, mothers, or big dreamers.


My mission

To be unapologetically, choatically me and encourage others to do the same while learning how to program for you and build for you knowing that nothing is truly accomplished without love and we love it here (insert crying face because still google how to center a div) empowering others to believe, build, and bloom for themselves — whether in tech or in life


What you’ll find here

You will find a place to be vulnerable and share your low moments in code as well as you high moments in code. A place to share your journey, your struggles, your wins, and your dreams. A place to learn, grow, and reach others in a powerful, encouraging way. I’ll share my journey, tips for beginners, and how to land that first dev job when the time comes. I’ll also share resources, tools, and inspiration to help you on your own path.


PS:

If you made it this far — THANK YOU. I’m rooting for you, and I hope you’ll root for me too. Let’s build something beautiful — one line of code at a time.
